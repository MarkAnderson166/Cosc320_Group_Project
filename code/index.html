How To Use

1.

Ask Student Services for your student CSV file, and upload it below.

2.

Press "Submit" for a calendar containing your completed units, and a few lists of units you still need to do.

3.

You can then drag units into your preferred trimesters.

Or, you can look at the recommended roadmap by pressing "Recommended".
